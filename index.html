Our Approach

Measured advice. Decisive action. Trusted representation.

The firm is a medium-sized boutique law firm based in Nairobi, Kenya, providing focused legal advisory and dispute resolution services in commercial, property, regulatory, and cross-border matters.

The firm acts for local and international clients, as well as foreign counsel, in matters requiring a precise understanding of Kenyan law, procedure, and enforcement mechanisms. Our approach emphasizes rigorous legal analysis, strategic foresight, and practical execution—whether advising on transactions, managing legal risk, or conducting litigation and alternative dispute resolution.

International Collaboration

The firm regularly partners with international law firms on cross-border matters, including Lai & Turner PLLC (United States) in relation to immigration and mobility-related legal work. These collaborations enable the firm to support clients whose legal needs span multiple jurisdictions while ensuring alignment with applicable local and foreign legal requirements.

Technology, Efficiency & Sustainability

The firm employs advanced case management and document-handling systems that significantly reduce reliance on physical files and paper-based processes. Through these systems, the firm has reduced its printing requirements by approximately 79%, in line with its broader commitment to tree conservation, environmental responsibility, and climate-conscious legal practice. This technology-driven approach enhances efficiency, accuracy, and responsiveness while supporting sustainable professional operations.

How We Help

Client pathways

Commercial & Corporate

Our commercial practice focuses on structuring and reviewing transactions, managing contractual and regulatory risk, and advising clients on governance and compliance issues affecting their operations in Kenya.

Dispute Resolution

We represent clients in litigation and alternative dispute resolution proceedings before Kenyan courts and tribunals. Our work emphasizes early issue identification, strategic case management, and preparation of matters for hearing, settlement, or enforcement.

Property, Construction & Real Estate

Our practice covers transactional, advisory, and contentious aspects of land, construction, and real estate, including acquisitions, financing, development projects, construction disputes, and property-related litigation within Kenya’s land registration and regulatory systems.

Immigration Law

In collaboration with international counsel, the firm advises on immigration and mobility matters affecting individuals and businesses, including cross-border relocation, regulatory compliance, and immigration-related dispute support.

Environmental & Climate Law

The firm advises on environmental and regulatory compliance matters, including issues relating to environmental governance, land use, sustainability obligations, and climate-related regulatory frameworks affecting projects and operations in Kenya.
