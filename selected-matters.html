Selected Matters (Illustrative)

Representative experience across commercial advisory, disputes, property, regulatory, construction, and cross-border matters. Client identities are withheld for confidentiality.

  • Commercial advisory: drafting and negotiation support for corporate contracting, governance, and risk allocation.
  • Property and conveyancing: due diligence, completion management, leasing, and dispute prevention documentation.
  • Regulatory: compliance advisory, stakeholder correspondence, and administrative decision strategy.
  • Construction: contractor/consultancy agreements, payment security provisions, and project risk advisory.
  • Disputes: injunction applications; debt recovery; contractual disputes; and structured settlement negotiations.
  • Immigration coordination: document bundles and declaration support in collaboration with overseas immigration counsel.

Further detail can be provided to instructing counsel upon request and subject to conflict checks.
